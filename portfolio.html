Projects I've contributed to.

  • Fluent Repos - system-icons, emoji, emoji-animated
    Code and asset repositories that I've maintained for the Fluent Design System at Microsoft. Icon merges are completed religiously every two weeks.
  • Icon Scaling Tool
    Publically available Figma plugin that let's users scale their icons and export them in SVG and PDF types. I maintain and add new features. It's currently used by our designers at Microsoft to export iconography to the public and to other internal teams.
  • Flubert
    Internal Figma at Microsoft that allows designers to use an LLM to analyze UI/UX in Figma. The LLM adheres to guidelines and principles of the Fluent Design System. I worked on creating an index trained on metaphors for Emoji and Icons.
  • Fluent Emoji Website
    Not maintained. Project for our UXE summer intern in 2023. Contributed to the sites main functionality and helped manage it's progress to ensure our intern's success.
  • GOT 'EM
    Weekend hack, and still running somehow. Nike SNKRS meme generator. I'm not sure if the "GOT 'EM" page has been updated since I made this site. If I could build dumb meme generators for the rest of my life, I'd be a happy man. I enjoyed deconstructing the success page and letting people create their own versions of it. Wrote about it here.
  • xComp Analytics
    Worked on a small team to build a software suite for Eastern Carolina University. The suite was designed to help students and faculty track their progress and success in their academic careers. I worked on the front-end and back-end of the project.
  • Hire Harlem
    Defunct. A project that I volunteered dev time to while I lived in the Bay Area. Hire Harlem was a website that spotlighted minority-owned businesses in Harlem, NY, and Oakland, CA. I wrote about the site redesign here.
  • fav-music-of-2018
    Glitch app on my favorite music of 2018! Includes little blurbs and links to the albums on iTunes and Spotify. Remixed from Tiffany Tseng
