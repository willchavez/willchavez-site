Fluent Repos

At Microsoft, I've maintained and created repositories and npm packages for icons, emoji, and animated-assets.

  1. Fluent System Icons (github, react-icons, svg-icons)

    Updates and fixes for multiple platforms were provided for this library (Android/iOS/react/react-native). Image analysis on updated icons was added as part of our scripting process.
  2. Fluent Emoji (github)

    This work was part of an effort to open source our Emoji library via Figma and Github! Our CVP of Design & Research featured our work in the medium article, Designing in the Open(Source).
  3. Fluent Emoji Animated (github)

    Created an open source repo for our animated emoji! Emoji are formatted as APNG files. Our custom tooling used apng assembler, imagemagick, & node js.
